# Peckish — itch.io page copy

## Tagline

A killdeer on the tideline: peck worms, dodge the surf, hatch your flock.

## Description

You are a killdeer — a little shorebird that sprints, stops, and pecks.
The richest worms are in the wet band every receding wave leaves behind,
so the best food is always exactly where the danger just was. Your
metabolism never stops burning: walk, sprint, limp, or wade, the energy
bar only goes down — eating is the only way up, and zero ends the run.

Between waves the beach is out to get you: crabs race you to every worm
and pinch when you're careless, a gull circles as a shadow with its eye on
your chicks, and an oblivious jogger comes pounding down a telegraphed
lane. Fight back the killdeer way — flip crabs onto their backs, crank
out a broken-wing act to lure the gull into an empty dive, and brood dune
eggs into a chick flock that trails behind you, hoovers up worms, and
multiplies every point you score.

## Features

- One-more-run arcade foraging with a real shorebird's risk economy —
  the best worms grow where the waves just hit
- Crank-powered broken-wing display, straight from actual killdeer
  behavior: fake an injury, bait the gull, dodge the dive for big points
- A flock to raise: brood eggs, keep chicks clear of gulls and surf, and
  stack a +25%-per-chick score multiplier
- Four hazards with fair telegraphs: surging (and sneaky sneaker) waves,
  worm-stealing crabs, a diving gull, one very oblivious jogger
- Escalating difficulty, saved high score, all-synth seaside sound
- Original 1-bit art: patterned water, foam, and a properly two-banded
  killdeer

## Controls

- **D-pad** — scurry
- **B (hold)** — sprint (burns energy faster)
- **A** — peck (eat worms; flip crabs, peck again to finish)
- **Crank** — broken-wing display (slow, but lures the gull onto you)

## Installing

No dev tools needed:

1. Download `Peckish.pdx.zip` from the Releases page (or `dist/`).
2. **On a Playdate:** upload it at https://play.date/account/sideload/
   then sync from Settings on the device.
3. **No Playdate?** Unzip and open `Peckish.pdx` in the Playdate
   Simulator (free with the Playdate SDK at https://play.date/dev/).
